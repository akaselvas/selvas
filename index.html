about us

We're a creative design studio that infuses authenticity, simplicity and boldness into new and established brands. From cultural institutions to consumer goods to tech, companies love collaborating with us because we listen carefully, think strategically and design thoughtfully.

SELVAS was founded in 2010 by Fabio Alves. We've combined decades of marketing expertise from New York and LA's top ad agencies with our passion for visual design, forming an intelligent and creatively-focused branding studio. We've built a reputation for bringing perfectionism, positive results and a unique design style to every company we work with.
